What We Do

We open doors to technology for young learners aged 9 to 18. By offering exposure and opportunities to those who lack them, we empower kids to build a brighter future and unlock their full potential in the digital world.

Our Recent Projects

Immersion and Inclusivity

In our first project, we connected with learners at MSDP who were new to computers. We began by introducing them to the basics, ensuring they felt comfortable and confident before advancing to more complex skills. This foundational support set the stage for their future growth in technology.

Laptop Initiative For Learners

Our current project focuses on sourcing laptops for our learners. While our dedicated tutors provide laptops, we are actively seeking additional funding to ensure every student has access to the tools they need. Your support can help us bridge the digital divide and enhance learning opportunities for all.

Build Websites

Once our learners were comfortable with basic computer skills, we guided them in creating their own simple websites. Each group designed a site related to their courses, applying their newfound skills in a practical and creative way. This hands-on project fostered confidence and demonstrated the power of technology.
