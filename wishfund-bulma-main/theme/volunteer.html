Be a Volunteer

We need your help

Join us in our quest to empower and ignite a passion for coding in young minds.
More tech minds mean a better world!

At LEA Foundation, we believe in the transformative power of education and technology. By volunteering with us, you become a vital part of our effort to equip underserved children with essential digital skills and inspire a lifelong love of learning. Your support can make a meaningful difference in a child's life, helping them unlock new opportunities and reach their full potential. Join us in creating a brighter future and empowering the next generation through education and technology.

Volunteer benefits

As a volunteer you will get benefit from:

  • Training - get discounted rates on our unique training courses.
  • Events - receive discounted tickets and invitations to member-only events.
  • Community - share knowledge and best practice with peers.
  • Networking - talk to the right people when you need to.
  • Expertise - access the Bond groups and the best tools and resources.
