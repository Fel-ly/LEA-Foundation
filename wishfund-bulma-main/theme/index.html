Provide Empowerment Through Technology

At the LEA Foundation, we empower young learners through hands-on coding workshops, collaborative community partnerships, and dedicated mentorship, transforming their potential into real-world technological skills.

What We Can Do

We can open doors to technology for young learners aged 9 to 18. By offering exposure and opportunities to those who lack them, we empower kids to build a brighter future and unlock their full potential in the digital world.

Our Recent Projects

Immersion and Inclusivity

In our first project, we connected with learners at MSDP who were new to computers. We began by introducing them to the basics, ensuring they felt comfortable and confident before advancing to more complex skills. This foundational support set the stage for their future growth in technology.

Laptop Initiative For Learners

Our current project focuses on sourcing laptops for our learners. While our dedicated tutors provide laptops, we are actively seeking additional funding to ensure every student has access to the tools they need. Your support can help us bridge the digital divide and enhance learning opportunities for all.

Build Websites

Once our learners were comfortable with basic computer skills, we guided them in creating their own simple websites. Each group designed a site related to their courses, applying their newfound skills in a practical and creative way. This hands-on project fostered confidence and demonstrated the power of technology.

Every Learner Deserves a Chance to Shine with Technology

When given the opportunity, every learner can explore and embrace technology. Providing access and exposure to tech tools opens doors to new possibilities, enabling young minds to discover their potential and pursue their passions. At LEA Foundation, we believe in creating these opportunities to transform lives and empower future innovators.

June, 2024

A Safe Space for New Beginnings and Eager Coders

At LEA Foundation, we create a nurturing environment where learners at Mukuru Slums Development Project feel secure and inspired to explore technology. Our safe space encourages curiosity and fosters a passion for coding. The enthusiasm of our young learners to dive into programming reflects the transformative power of providing access and support.

June, 2024

Building a School to Empower Underserved Children

Our vision extends beyond just providing technology education; we are committed to creating a dedicated learning space for children from underprivileged backgrounds. By building a school, we aim to offer a stable environment where these children can access quality education and technology training.
