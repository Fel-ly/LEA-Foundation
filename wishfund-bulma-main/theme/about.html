About Us

Welcome to LEA Foundation

Where we learn about technology whilst having fun!

The LEA Foundation is dedicated to creating awareness and empowering learners with basic technological knowledge and essential coding skills. This is achieved through practical training and hands-on projects. The trainers are very knowledgeable and highly conversant with technology, making them well-suited to help us achieve our goal of getting more people to learn about coding.

Mission


To ignite a passion for coding in high school students through dynamic and inclusive learning experiences, preparing them for the opportunities and challenges of the digital age.

Vision


A future where all students, regardless of background, have the skills and confidence to thrive in a technology-driven world, contributing to a more inclusive and innovative society.
